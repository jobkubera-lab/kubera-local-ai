# Email to Employ Merton

**Subject:** Resident-led digital employment support idea for Merton

Dear Employ Merton Team,

My name is Mykola Shcheglov. I am a new resident in Mitcham and I have been using Merton Council services to understand local employment, support and community opportunities.

I am currently studying AI tools, digital services and employment support. I would like to share a small resident-led idea that may support new residents and jobseekers in Merton:

**Merton Accessible Career & Welcome Map** — a simple digital prototype that could help residents find local job support, understand first steps, discover volunteering and apprenticeship routes, and access useful community services.

The prototype could include:

1. A local support map showing Employ Merton, libraries, Merton Vision, community organisations, volunteering routes and adult learning.

2. A new-resident employment checklist covering right to work, National Insurance, UK-style CVs, job search, libraries and training.

3. A simple AI-style assistant that explains job descriptions, CV preparation, cover letters and interview preparation in plain English.

4. Multilingual support ideas for residents whose first language is not English.

I would be happy to share a small demo page and discuss whether this could be useful as a volunteer contribution, remote support activity, part-time opportunity, apprenticeship pathway or small project collaboration.

Kind regards,

Mykola Shcheglov  
Mitcham resident
