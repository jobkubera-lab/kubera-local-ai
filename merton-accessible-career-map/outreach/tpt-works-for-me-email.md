# Email to Thomas Pocklington Trust / Works For Me

**Subject:** Accessible AI and digital job-search support idea for Works For Me

Dear Thomas Pocklington Trust Employment Team,

My name is Mykola Shcheglov. I am a new resident in Merton and I am currently studying AI tools, digital services and employment support.

I recently learned about your Works For Me employment programme and the support you provide to blind and partially sighted people who are looking for paid employment or a career change.

I would like to share a small resident-led idea that may support your employment work: an accessible AI and digital job-search support prototype.

The idea could include:

1. Simple guidance on how jobseekers can use AI tools such as ChatGPT or Claude to prepare CVs, cover letters and interview answers.

2. An accessible local employment support map for Merton and South London, showing organisations such as Merton Vision, Employ Merton, libraries, adult learning, volunteering and community services.

3. A step-by-step checklist for new residents or people returning to work, including right to work, CV preparation, job search, reasonable adjustments and Access to Work.

4. Multilingual support ideas for people whose first language is not English.

I would be happy to share a small demo or discuss whether this could be useful as a volunteer contribution, workshop, remote support activity or future collaboration.

Kind regards,

Mykola Shcheglov  
Mitcham / Merton resident
