# Merton Accessible Career & Welcome Map

Resident-led digital prototype for **Merton Vision**, **TPT Works For Me** and **Employ Merton**.

This is **not an official Merton Council, Merton Vision or Thomas Pocklington Trust service**. It is a small civic-tech demo showing how a new resident in Mitcham/Merton could find local employment support, accessibility support, digital help and first-step guidance.

## Purpose

The prototype shows how a simple digital assistant + local map could help people who are:

- new residents in Merton;
- looking for work, apprenticeships or volunteering;
- blind or partially sighted;
- returning to work after health or life disruption;
- people with international experience;
- people whose first language is not English.

Merton Council's employment and recruitment service helping residents find work, training and job support.

- Outreach letters for Merton Vision, TPT Works For Me and Employ Merton.
- Proposal document for a small pilot project.

## Suggested first pilot

A small, low-risk pilot for Mitcham/Merton:

1. Build a simple web map of local support organisations.
2. Add a new-resident checklist.
3. Add a job-search checklist.
4. Add accessible AI guidance for CVs, cover letters and interviews.
5. Test with community organisations before any official use.

## Privacy and safety principles

- No personal data stored in the demo.
- No login required.
- No medical or legal advice.
- Clear signposting to official services.
- Human review for any real deployment.
- Accessibility first: large text, high contrast, keyboard support, screen-reader labels.
