# Proposal: Merton Accessible Career & Welcome Map

## One-line idea

A small accessible digital map and AI-style guidance prototype helping new residents, jobseekers and blind or partially sighted people in Merton find employment support, community services and digital help.

## Why this matters

Many residents do not start with a clear understanding of the local support system. They may not know which organisation to contact first, what documents they need, how to prepare a UK-style CV, how to ask for reasonable adjustments, or how to find local job and digital support.

A simple, accessible digital pathway can reduce confusion and help residents reach the right service faster.

## Target users

- New residents in Merton.
- Residents looking for work or apprenticeships.
- People with international experience.
- Blind and partially sighted residents.
- People returning to work after health issues or life disruption.
- Residents with limited English.

## Partner organisations

### Merton Vision

Local sight-loss charity in Merton. Potential angle: accessible digital support, AI tools for CVs/interviews, local support map, volunteering.

### Thomas Pocklington Trust / Works For Me

Employment programme for blind and partially sighted people. Potential angle: accessible AI job-search support and workshop material.

### Employ Merton

Merton Council employment and recruitment service. Potential angle: new-resident employment pathway, local job support map, digital inclusion.

## Prototype features

1. **Local support map**
   - Merton Vision
   - Employ Merton
   - Libraries
   - Community organisations
   - Volunteering
   - Online employment support

2. **New resident pathway**
   - Right to work
   - National Insurance
   - GP registration
   - Library and digital support
   - Job support
   - English/adult learning

3. **Accessible employment pathway**
   - CV and cover letter support
   - Interview preparation
   - Reasonable adjustments
   - Access to Work signposting
   - Screen-reader-friendly checklists

4. **AI tools guidance**
   - Safe use of ChatGPT/Claude for job search
   - Privacy warnings
   - Plain-English explanations
   - Human review for important decisions

## Pilot plan

### Phase 1: Demo

Create a static website showing the idea with no personal data and no login.

### Phase 2: Community feedback

Share with Merton Vision, Employ Merton and TPT Works For Me for feedback.

### Phase 3: Workshop idea

Offer a small online or in-person session: "Using AI tools safely for CVs, cover letters and interviews."

### Phase 4: Improved prototype

Refine the map, add verified service data and improve accessibility.

## Accessibility principles

- Large readable text.
- High contrast.
- Keyboard navigation.
- Screen-reader labels.
- No hidden forms.
- Clear official links.
- No collection of sensitive personal data.

## Important disclaimer

This is a resident-led prototype. It is not an official service and should not provide legal, medical or benefits advice. Any official deployment would need organisational review, safeguarding checks, data protection review and accessibility testing.
